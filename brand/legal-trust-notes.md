# TubeBoard Legal And Trust Notes

This is production guidance, not legal advice.

## TfL And Trademark

- TubeBoard must not state or imply that it is official, endorsed, sponsored, approved by, or affiliated with Transport for London.
- Do not use the TfL roundel or London Underground roundel as the app icon, brand mark, website logo, or campaign logo without written permission.
- Do not recreate official station signage, maps, or lockups in a way that could confuse users.
- Tube line colours may be used for functional line identification, but should not form the primary brand mark.

## Suggested Disclaimer

TubeBoard is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport for London. Live arrival information is powered by TfL Open Data where available.

Confirm final wording against TfL Open Data terms before launch.

## App Store Trust

- Do not show unimplemented features in final App Store screenshots.
- Roadmap concepts should be kept out of launch metadata unless clearly marked in internal docs only.
- If subscriptions, trials, or paid features are added, metadata must describe them accurately.
- Any location, analytics, crash reporting, or personal-data behavior must match the privacy label.

## Data Source

Owner confirmation required:
- Exact TfL Open Data attribution wording.
- API reliability/freshness language.
- Whether the app caches arrivals.
- Whether data appears when TfL services are unavailable.

## Privacy

Owner confirmation required:
- Does the app request location permission?
- Does it store favourite stations locally only?
- Does it use analytics or crash reporting?
- Does it collect identifiers?
- What is the final privacy policy URL?

## Website And Press

- Include independent disclaimer in footer and press kit.
- Avoid official station sign imagery unless licensed or clearly generic.
- Avoid phrases like “official Tube board” or “TfL app.”
- Use “London Tube arrivals” descriptively, not as an endorsement claim.

## Owner Decisions

- Final app name/title.
- Support URL and contact email.
- Privacy policy URL.
- Monetization/pricing copy.
- Final implemented feature list for App Store screenshots.
- Final data attribution wording.
