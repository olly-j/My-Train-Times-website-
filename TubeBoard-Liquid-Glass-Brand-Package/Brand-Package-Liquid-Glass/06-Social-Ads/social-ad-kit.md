# TubeBoard Social And Advertising Kit

## Campaign Platform

Campaign idea: **Open the board. Pick your train.**

Key visual: a black glass iPhone showing an amber departure board, with subtle line-colour traces and LED train-light dots moving behind it.

Messaging hierarchy:
1. Live Tube arrivals.
2. Seconds-based countdowns.
3. Favourite stations.
4. Platform-board clarity.
5. Independent, transparent data source.

Brand promise: TubeBoard gives you the next Tube without route-planner friction.

Target audiences:
- Daily commuters checking regular stations.
- Londoners changing lines on the move.
- Visitors comfortable using the Tube but needing live arrivals.
- iPhone users who prefer focused utility apps.

CTA:
- Coming to the App Store.
- Join the launch list.
- Open the board.
