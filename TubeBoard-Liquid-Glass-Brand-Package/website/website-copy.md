## FAQ

Is TubeBoard official?
No. TubeBoard is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Transport for London.

Does it use live data?
TubeBoard is designed to use TfL Open Data for live arrivals where available.

Can I save my usual stations?
Yes, favourites are part of the core product direction. Confirm the final launch implementation before App Store screenshots are submitted.

Will there be widgets or Live Activities?
Treat widgets, Live Activities, Dynamic Island, Apple Watch, and alerts as roadmap-only unless they are implemented in the launch build.
